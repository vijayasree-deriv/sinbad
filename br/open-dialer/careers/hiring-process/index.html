Our hiring process
Send curriculum vitae (CV)
Send your CV and a cover letter to hr@sinbad.software to get started.
1
Self-assessment questionnarie
We'll send you a questionnaire to help us learn more about you.
2
Interview with HR and Manager
An HR representative and your potential manager will interview you.
3
Interview with COO/CTO/CEO
In this round, you'll speak with our COO, CTO or CEO depending on the role.
4
Background and reference check
Upon approval, our team will do a background and reference check.
5
Job offer
If everything goes well, you'll recieve a job offer. Be ready, as your new adventure with us is about to begin.
6
You're one of us
Welcome to Sinbad! You'll recieve your start date and the details of your onboarding programme.
7
