Why work at sinbad software?

Fintech is an industry where projects come in many shapes and sizes. You’ll be constantly challenged to come up with new solutions and work with various technologies. With us, you’ll have the opportunity to continuously learn and build your skills while working on new challenges with a smart, supportive team.

Helping you grow in your career

We offer a robust training programme to help you with seamless onboarding when you join us. Depending on your needs, your team lead might provide you with technical training, too. Most importantly, we have an ongoing education assistance programme — applicable to both short-term and long-term work-related courses — to encourage colleagues to boost their knowledge and skills. Lateral moving is also a practice that we warmly welcome as a part of your career development as it can broaden and deepen your experience.

Our hiring process

Send curriculum vitae (CV)
Send your CV and a cover letter to hr@sinbad.software to get started.
1
Self-assessment questionnarie
We'll send you a questionnaire to help us learn more about you.
2
Interview with HR and Manager
An HR representative and your potential manager will interview you. They will assess your experience, skills, and cultural fit.
3
Interview with COO/CTO/CEO
In this round, you'll speak with our COO, CTO, or CEO depending on the role.
4
Background and reference check
Upon approval, our team will do a background and reference check.
5
Job offer
If everything goes well, you'll receive a job offer. Be ready, as your new adventure with us is about to begin.
6
You're one of us
Welcome to Sinbad! You'll receive your start date and the details of your onboarding programme.
